You decide to inspect the body. The head looks severed and the mouth of the thing is covered in blood. It's eyes are bloodshot red and it looks like it's skin is literally rotting around it's head, barely keeping together. what... IS this thing? You ask yourself. Upon inspection of the body you find similar traits. Skin rotting off, etc.

You think of your options. You can look around the room for hidden doors, storage compartments, or for equipment. You can also check the bodies and see what they have on them.
