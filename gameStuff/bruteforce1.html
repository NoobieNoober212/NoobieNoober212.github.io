Testing out the door, you decide that a good kick close to the door knob would break it open. You back up a step and kick the door as hard as possible. Luckily, the door is weak and you are able to kick it open. Looking inside, the first thing you see is a cloaked figure hunched over what looks another body. The sudden sound alerts the figure and it instantly lets out waht can only be described an an inhumane screech. It turns around and instantly runs towards you.
