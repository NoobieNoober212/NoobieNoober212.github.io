Gripping the sword tight in your hand, you sprint, overwhelmed with panick and the pure desire to escape this... place. Seeing a doorway you sprint through it and suddenly... Your feet miss the ground. You let out a yelp as your body twists in the air and you see another doorway on the right side of the hallway you just left. Looking down you see a dark abyss of nothingness. A dark abyss you're falling down. You let out a scream as finaly, the ground comes into view, fast. Too fast. Then, nothing.
