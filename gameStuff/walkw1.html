Panick starts to set in at the sight of so much blood. You stop walking and try to compose yourself. After a few minutes you feel better and spot an door aswell as an archway leading into a very dark room. Deciding not to go through you walk open the door and attempt to open the door. It appears locked. You have two options. Go check out the opening, or try to bruteforce the door open.
