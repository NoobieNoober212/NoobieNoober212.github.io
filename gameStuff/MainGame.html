  • Take the sword

You wake up in a cold cave with torches hung up on the walls. In the middle of the room is a large sword embeded in stone. Looking around you there is a what appears to be a doorway going into a dark hallway with less candles lit up. A shiver goes up your spine and you debate wether trying to take the sword with you. You have no memory of what happened but all you know is that you are here, now.
